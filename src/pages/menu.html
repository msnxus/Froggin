FROGGIN

Jump from lily pad to lily pad to earn points!

How to play:

  • Move and turn froggy using the ARROW KEYS.
  • Press the SPACEBAR to jump. Holding will allow you to jump further.
  • Press F to enter first person mode. Use WASD to look around.
  • If you miss a lily pad, you killed Froggy.
Press P to play!
